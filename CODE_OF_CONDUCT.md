# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)
(v2.1).

In short: be respectful, welcoming, and constructive. Harassment, discrimination,
and personal attacks are not tolerated. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject
contributions and comments that violate this code, and may ban contributors for
inappropriate behaviour.

To report unacceptable behaviour, contact the maintainer (see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)
for a private channel). Reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.
